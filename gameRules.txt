Blackjack game rules and features:

- This is a 1 player game (for now) + dealer (who plays too)
- Player can make a bet (player bet equal to dealer bet) // not sure about that
(20, 50, 100, 200, 500, 1000, max)
- Player can add money onclick
- Player options:  
    -PASS/STAND (enough cards), 
    -HIT (add one more card),
    -DOUBLE/X2 (double bet + add exactly one card), 
    -SPLIT (avaible if two cards has equal signs , split your hand into two with x2 bet, all other options still avaible)
- No time limits for turn?
- U can play with 1-6 decks of cards
- if no cards in deck -> add new deck
